Byung-Kuk Seo
Senior Researcher
ETRI
Current appointment
Senior Researcher/Permanent Member in CG/Vision Research Team of Communication Media Research Lab. at Electronics and Telecommunications Research Institute (ETRI)

I earned my Ph.D. in Electronics and Computer Engineering at Hanyang University under the supervision of Prof. Jong-Il Park. During my Ph.D., I had the good fortune to collaborate with Dr. Slobodan Ilic in Chair for Computer Aided Medical Procedures & Augmented Reality (CAMP) at Technical University of Munich (TUM). Before joining the ETRI, I was a postdoc in Mixed Reality Lab. at Hanyang University under the guidance of Prof. Jong-Il Park. And then, I moved to Dept. of Virtual and Augmented Reality at Fraunhofer Institute for Computer Graphics (IGD) and worked as a postdoctoral research fellow under the guidance of Dr. Harald Wuest.
